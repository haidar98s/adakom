Adakom International Technology

Delivering superior quality products

PT Adakom International Technology established in 2004 as a distribution company providing server case, power supplies and accessories. Located at CBD Pluit - DKI Jakarta, Indonesia, our company is an authorized distributor for Dell, HP, ASUS and the sole distributor of Agile products. Agile is our local brand for products such as chassis, power supply, and etc which are selected by us from the manufacturer.

Our company is committed to offer the right products with competitive price and to provide up to-date product information. PT Adakom International Technology has been delivering superior quality products to customers with assurance that all standard products meet customers demands.
